Sri Prakash Educational Institutes, established in the year 1977 has been very popular since 44 years today and has grown steadily with 9 campuses in Tuni, Payakaraopeta, Vizag, Peddapuram and Rajamundry with its campuses being spread across 120 acres and functional area of 8,00,000 sqft. Sri Prakash Institutes are committed to provide quality education with world class infrastructure and vast 25 acre play area for sports and games making it the Best All Round Education School in Andhra Pradesh.

Our academic program helps the children build a broader thought process and increase their problem solving skills alongside increasing their confidence. We ensure that the classes are more interactive where children can ask more questions, use their sentence formation skills to speak/write in their own words. Enjoy working on a lot of word problems in mathematics such that they are able to read in between the lines. We help children grow in a way that they are independent and can complete their homework with very little to no help from their teachers. We ensure that they are very comfortable in speaking English and score exceptionally well on tests.
